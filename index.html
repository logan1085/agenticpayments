Agentic Commerce Market Map April 2026

v2 · Based on Artemis LandscapeCurated by Logan Horowitz

Two distinct markets are forming. A human-in-the-loop market (arriving now, B2C first) that improves discovery and checkout with a user still pressing the final button, and an autonomous agent market (being built, B2B first) where agents decide, pay, and execute independently on net-new infrastructure. They share foundation models and stablecoins but diverge in rails, identity, and UX.
